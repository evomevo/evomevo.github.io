Introduction


Welcome to my website! You might know me as the founder and owner of wedery, a YouTube channel about Meteorology that I launched in 2021. I do multiple forms of art, which are listed below. I'm an introverted person, though I am open to proper discussions. I'm interested in lounge and trap/hip hop instrumental music, many studies of science (especially Meteorology, Astronomy, and Technology), and more.

Motion Design / Video Editing


I've used many video editors, including Windows' built-in one, with each giving me a sense of what I can do with sending a message through many forms of media. Since I was interested in Meteorology, and with many YouTube channels giving me inspiration, it gave me strength to start a YouTube channel in April of 2021, where I can share my talents, knowledge, and much more through the platform to the worldwide community.

Graphics Design


Ever since I discovered it in 2020, Figma has enabled me to find my other talent with graphics design, I was creating fun stuff like icons, logos, satire image editing, and much more during my free time. I also create cyclone updates (mainly for the Philippines) on Twitter under wedery, massively helped by the Auto Layout feature. What we do may even indirectly save lives, who knows?

Photography


Seeing architectural wonders of mankind, and the beautiful designs of nature, has piqued my interest in taking photos. So I decided to share some of my best ones through twitter.
