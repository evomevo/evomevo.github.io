Introduction


Welcome to my website! You might know me as the founder and owner of wedery, a YouTube channel about Meteorology that I launched in 2021. I do multiple forms of art, which are listed below. I'm an introverted person, though I am open to proper discussions. I like French House and Future Funk music, and I usually prefer instrumentals or ones with vocal cuts. I'm also interested in many studies of science, such as Meteorology, Astronomy, and Technology, and more.

Motion Design


I've used many video editors in my lifetime, including Windows' built-in one, with each giving me a sense of what I can do with sending a message through many forms of media. Since I was interested in Meteorology, and with many YouTube channels giving me inspiration, it gave me strength to start a YouTube channel in April of 2021, where I can share many things through the platform.

Graphics Design


Ever since I discovered it in 2020, Figma has enabled me to find my other talent with graphics design, I create stuff like icons, logos, banners, and much more for wedery, for some people, or for fun. There's many random projects/stuff that haven't been released yet

Web Design


This is actually one of my more recent stuff I learned, and I'm already decent at it! Creating this portfolio website was actually my learning experience in web design. I also create cyclone updates (mainly for the North Pacific) on Twitter under wedery, mainly created using the Auto Layout feature of Figma, which is actually just an easier version of CSS! Pretty cool, huh?

Photography


Seeing architectural wonders of mankind, and the beautiful designs of nature, has piqued my interest in taking photos. So I decided to share some of my best ones through twitter.
